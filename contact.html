Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take? ▼
It depends on the project scope. A standard website typically takes 2–4 weeks from kickoff to launch. AI automation projects take 1–3 weeks. More complex generative AI solutions may take 4–8 weeks. We'll give you a precise timeline after our discovery call.
Do you work with businesses outside Bengaluru? ▼
Absolutely. We work with clients across India — remotely, with full transparency and regular communication. Our team is comfortable handling projects entirely online, with video calls, shared project boards, and real-time updates.
What is included in your maintenance packages? ▼
Our maintenance packages include regular software/plugin updates, security monitoring and patching, performance optimisation, daily backups, uptime monitoring, and up to 2 hours of content updates per month. Custom packages are available based on your needs.
Do I need to know anything about AI to use your AI services? ▼
Not at all. We handle all the technical complexity. You just need to tell us your business goals and pain points — we'll design the right AI solution and train your team to use it confidently. No technical knowledge required.
What happens after the project is delivered? ▼
Every project includes a free support period (1–6 months depending on package). After that, you can opt into one of our maintenance or retainer plans. We also offer training so your team can manage the day-to-day independently.
How do you handle payments and contracts? ▼
We provide a clear project agreement before any work begins. Payment is typically split: 50% upfront, 50% on completion. We accept UPI, bank transfer, and other standard payment methods. Everything is transparent with no hidden fees.
