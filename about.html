How We Started

Born from a Belief That Technology Should Work For You

Stackmin was founded in 2025 in Bengaluru by two young innovators who believed that small and medium businesses in India deserved access to the same world-class digital tools that large corporations enjoy — without the enterprise price tag.

We saw businesses struggling with outdated websites, manual repetitive tasks, and no clear path to leveraging AI. So we built a company to change that. Stackmin bridges the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical, affordable business solutions.

Today, we work with businesses across Karnataka and beyond, delivering custom web solutions and intelligent AI automations that create real, measurable impact.
